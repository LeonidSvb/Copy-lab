# SSM SOP — Connector Placeholder Prompts & Opener Rotation

Source: SSM (mentor materials)

This SOP gives you the exact anti-fragile prompts that position you as the connector —
someone with access, signal, and knowledge of the pull.

## Variables used across prompts

- `[dreamICP]` — plural ICP group written the way operators talk: "founders in logistics", "CTOs in SaaS", "HR directors in manufacturing"
- `[painTheySolve]` — how they naturally complain: "can't keep up with hiring", "waste hours on back-and-forth"
- `[clean_company_name]` — company name WITHOUT LLC/Inc/Corp/Ltd
- `[job_titles]` — real titles: "CFOs", "store managers", "IT directors"
- `[company_type]` — specific: "mid-sized law firms", "Series A startups", "regional banks"
- `[pain_description]` — how they complain to a friend: "waste hours on", "can't find", "keep losing"

---

## PROMPT #1 — Company-Based Connector Insight

**Output format:**
```
Noticed [clean_company_name] helps [job_titles] at [company_type] — I know a few who [pain_description].
```

**Perfect example:**
> Noticed Summit Capital helps CEOs and CFOs at mid-market manufacturing companies — I know a few who can't find buyers when they're ready to exit and waste months with investment bankers who don't understand their industry.

**Rules:**
- ONE sentence only
- No corporate speak: avoid "solutions", "leverage", "optimize", "streamline", "platform"
- Write like the buyer would complain to a friend

---

## PROMPT #2 — Connector Insight from Market Conversations

**Output format:**
```
Figured I'd reach out — I talk to a lot of [dreamICP] and they keep saying they [painTheySolve].
Thought you two should connect.
```

**Perfect example:**
> Figured I'd reach out — I talk to a lot of CEOs in mid-market manufacturing and they keep saying they can't find buyers who actually understand their space.
> Thought you two should connect.

---

## PROMPT #3 — "I'm Around Them Daily" Spine

**Output format:**
```
Figured I'd reach out — I'm around [dreamICP] daily and they keep saying they [painTheySolve].
```

**Perfect example:**
> Figured I'd reach out — I'm around CEOs in mid-market manufacturing daily and they keep saying they can't find buyers who actually understand their space.

---

## PROMPT #4 — "Saw Some Movement on My Side" (Deal-Flow)

**Output format (3 lines):**
```
Saw some movement on my side —
Figured I'd reach out — I'm around [dreamICP] daily and they keep saying they [painTheySolve].
Can plug you into the deal flow if you want.
```

## PROMPT #5 — "Your Name Came Up On My End" (Ultra-Operator)

Same spine as #3, prefixed with "Your name came up on my end —"

---

## PROMPT #6 — "Got a Signal That Fits You" (Most Premium)

Same spine as #3, premium deal-flow energy.

---

## Opener Rotation Framework

Rotate the opener only — the core spine stays the same:

1. Cleanest, highest-status → `"Been mapping some signals lately —"`
2. I see the pull before others do → `"Saw some movement on my side —"`
3. Ultra-operator, minimal → `"Your name came up on my end —"`
4. Exclusive, deal-flow coded → `"Been curating a few matches this week —"`
5. Most premium, least effort → `"Got a signal that fits you —"`

---

## Follow-Up Sequence (Connector Edition)

**Follow-Up #1:**
> Hey {{firstName}}, worth intro'ing you?

**Follow-Up #2 (final):**
> Hey {{firstName}}, maybe this isn't something you're interested in — wishing you the best.

**Psychology:** Never break frame. Connectors don't chase. Same posture from touch 1 to close.
