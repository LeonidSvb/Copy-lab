# Niche Context: Recruiting / Staffing

## Who we are reaching out to
Owners, directors, and senior recruiters at recruiting and staffing agencies.

## Our value prop for this niche
We monitor the market for companies that are actively mid-search (need to hire right now).
We connect recruiting agencies to these companies — warm intros, no cold BD needed.

## The key distinction (CRITICAL for extraction)
Recruiting agencies have two groups:
- CANDIDATES = the people they place (software engineers, lawyers, accountants, etc.) — this is their PRODUCT
- CLIENTS/BUYERS = the companies that PAY them to find candidates (CTOs, hiring managers, founders, VPs, etc.) — this is their ICP

When extracting dreamICP, we want the CLIENTS/BUYERS, NOT the candidates.
