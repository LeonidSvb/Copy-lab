You are evaluating a cold email for quality. Score it strictly — be a tough critic.

Score each metric listed below. Return ONLY valid JSON, nothing else.

SCORING GUIDE:

specificity (0-5):
  5 = names a very specific type of company/person with a real pain
  3 = somewhat specific but could apply to many
  0 = completely generic, no real signal

genericness_penalty (0-5):
  0 = no generic phrases (GOOD)
  5 = full of "solutions", "leverage", "help you grow" (BAD)
  Penalize: "solutions", "optimize", "streamline", "leverage", "take your X to the next level"

clarity (0-5):
  5 = reads naturally in one pass, human tone
  0 = confusing, robotic, or awkward phrasing

role_confusion (0 or 1):
  1 = email confuses WHO the sender is talking to (e.g., implies sender works with candidates, not hiring managers)
  0 = roles are clear

length_violation (0 or 1):
  1 = icebreaker line is more than 2 sentences OR the full email is bloated
  0 = appropriate length

total_score = specificity + clarity - genericness_penalty - (role_confusion * 3) - (length_violation * 2)

issues = list of specific problems found (empty array if none)
verdict = one sentence on why this email wins or loses
