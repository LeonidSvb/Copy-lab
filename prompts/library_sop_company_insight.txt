# Angle 1 — Company-Based Connector Insight
# "Noticed [company] helps [X] at [Y] — I know a few who [pain]"

Write ONE cold email opener line using the company-based connector angle.

EXTRACTED VARIABLES:
- clean_company_name: {clean_company_name}
- dreamICP (job titles): {dream_icp_titles}
- company_type: {company_type}
- painTheySolve: {pain_they_solve}

OUTPUT FORMAT (follow this exactly):
Noticed [clean_company_name] helps [job_titles] at [company_type] — I know a few who [pain_description].

RULES:
- ONE sentence only
- [job_titles] = short plural titles: "CTOs", "plant managers", "general counsels" — NOT "decision-makers"
- [pain_description] = how they complain to a friend: "can't find", "keep losing", "waste weeks on"
- No corporate speak: no "solutions", "leverage", "optimize", "streamline"
- Must feel like a genuine observation, not a sales pitch
- No quotation marks around the output

Output ONLY the single sentence. Nothing else.
